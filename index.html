Mission & Vision

Mission: To empower the youth and community through education, sports, and social development programs.

Vision: A stronger, more connected Thulhaadhoo community built on teamwork, leadership, and shared success.

About Us

Target Community is a youth-led nonprofit organization based in Baa. Thulhaadhoo, Maldives. We organize community events, sports tournaments, and educational programs aimed at inspiring young people to contribute positively to society.
